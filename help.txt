There are some options here that you can choose. Below you can read about every one:
Create. You can add a new habit. For that you should write name, description, category and frequency of your new habit
Habits can belong to sport, study or hobby habits. Then you have to choose a frequency of a habit: daily (1) or weekly(7).

Complete. When a habit is added, you can complete it. When you choose this option, you will see a list of your added habits.

Analyse. Here you can see how many times has your habit been completed. You should also choose a habit from a list.

Delete. If you don't want to get a new habit, you can just delete it from a list.

List of habits. Here you can see the whole list of your habit with description, category and frequency.
